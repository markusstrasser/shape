The Shape Fellowship


The Shape Foundation is launching a fellowship to support exceptional upcoming engineers, researchers and founders in the biological and computer sciences.

The upcoming inaugural cohort of fellows will be invited to San Francisco for a full 2+ week program of get togethers, lab tours, journal clubs, group dinners, hikes, daytrips and meetups with leading deep tech researchers, engineers and founders. In short: phenomenal vibes.

The exact dates and durations are still TBA but will likely be either before or after the 2022 summer break so as to not clash with the usual dates for summer internships.

We plan to have recurring IRL events and retreats in and close to capitals around the world, depending on where fellows happen to be located.

Flights, chic housing close to Golden Gate park and a stipend to cover San Francisco expenses will be fully covered by the foundation. Other than vibeweek(s), we are keen to support fellows for personal explorations, open source contributions or, in some cases, company formation.


WHAT IS THE APPLICATION PROCESS?
We do two 30 minute video calls and one technical/brainstorming session to see if a potential fellow is a fit.


WHO's FUNDING THIS?
So far we have $1M in contributions from founders, VCs and philanthropists who are predominantly in the Bay Area and NYC.

INSPIRATIONS
The Shape fellowship is similar in spirit to the Emergent Ventures fellowship or NewScience but with a focus on open source software infrastructure and productionizing research into openly usable applications.
